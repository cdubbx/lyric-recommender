Common Themes in Song Lyrics:

- Love: Falling in love, being in love, or losing love.
- Heartbreak: Breakups, betrayal, loneliness.
- Growth: Personal development, learning from life.
- Party: Celebration, dancing, freedom.
- Rebellion: Breaking rules, independence, anti-authority.
- Mental health: Struggles with anxiety, depression, or healing.
- Social issues: Race, gender, politics, injustice.

Analyzing lyrics helps uncover the deeper emotional and thematic core of a song.
