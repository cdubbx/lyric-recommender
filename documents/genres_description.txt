Popular Music Genres and Their Characteristics:

- Pop: Catchy melodies, repetitive structure, hooks. Designed for mass appeal.
- Rock: Guitar-driven, often with powerful vocals. Subgenres include classic rock, punk, and metal.
- Hip-Hop: Rhythmic and lyrical, focuses on flow, rhyme, and beat. Often tells stories or expresses identity.
- R&B: Smooth vocals, soulful themes, emotional depth. Influenced by jazz and blues.
- EDM: Electronic production, danceable beats, drops and synths. Includes house, trance, dubstep.
- Country: Storytelling lyrics, acoustic instruments, often rooted in rural life and values.
- Indie: Diverse sound, often DIY-produced. Known for creativity and less commercial production.

Songs can belong to multiple genres and mix elements freely.
